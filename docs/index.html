Frequently Asked Questions

Liquidity has been locked for 6 months on team.finance and the developer renounced ownership after deploying the contract. No team or advisor wallets were generated.

In addition to this, contract ownership was renounced via the renounceOwnership() function making it a fully autonomous token with no control over it by any one party.

A small liquidity pool fundraiser (10ETH) was held with 0 tokens allocated to the team. This was locked in to UniSwap for at least 6 months.

  • 60% (3M) of all tokens were distributed during this fundraiser.
  • 40% (2M) of all tokens were locked to the UniSwap liquidity pool.

The reward mechanism built into the contract doesn’t distribute rewards via individual blockchain transactions, you will be able to see your current balance by viewing your address on the Etherscan website.
